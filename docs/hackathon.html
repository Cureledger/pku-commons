How to join

Two ways in, build remotely, or find us at the table.

  1. Fork the repo. Start from github.com/Cureledger/pku-commons and pick a target: the phe-estimator Skill, the food list, or the scale integration.
  2. Pick a problem worth solving. The suggested hacks on the home page map real PKU pain points to concrete builds.
  3. Score on the benchmark. Every change is measured on the public accuracy benchmark. Improvements merge only when they raise the score without regressing.
  4. Post your work in our Discord. Share what you built and the feedback you got. Team Phebe reviews entries and features them at the table.
  5. At the conference? Come by our table. Bring an app, bring a frustration, bring a recipe, and we'll hack on it with you on the spot.

Why hack in real time?

The hardest problems in phe estimation appear away from the lab, in a grocery aisle, in a home kitchen, at a birthday party. By building at the table, feedback from a family becomes a tested change the same day, and the next family to stop by sees it working.

Every improvement outlives the event. Changes merge into the open PKU Commons repos and are locked in by the public benchmark, so a tool keeps working, and keeps being trustworthy, long after the conference ends.
